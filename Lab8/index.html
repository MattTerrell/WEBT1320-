    New Releases will be coming to the consoles soon. The Xbox Series has several special exclusives such as Halo, Gears of War, and Crackdown. Halo Infinite will be released on the new Xbox series consoles in November. Until then players could play other exclusive xbox games like Master Chief collection or maybe even play an older game liked Left 4 Dead.

    The Console is very easy to set up. First all you have to do is plug with the power and HDMI cables. After that you can either put your previous gaming profile on the console or create a new one. If you have an Xbox One you can have both consoles on at once and transfer all data and games over or you can download them manually. It does take several hours for everything to be ready but the high quality of the systemmakes it worth it.

    If you have any questions feel free to log on to the Official Microsoft Website and chat with a represenative. They will be able to answer any question you may have about your new system.
